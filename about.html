My Story

How it all started

It started, like most good things do, with a one-way ticket and absolutely no plan. I didn't set out to become a travel content creator — I just wanted to see the world the way it actually was, not the polished, overpriced, tourist-trap version that most guides sell.

The Maldives was the turning point. Everyone told me it was only for honeymooners and billionaires. I found a tiny island called Gaafaru that barely showed up on Google Maps. Stayed with locals. Dove with whale sharks. Ate the best food I'd ever tasted for $5. And I thought — someone needs to write this down.

So I did. The Maldives Insider Guide became my first ebook, and the response was something I didn't expect. People wanted more. More destinations, more honest guides, more of the stuff Google doesn't surface.

Now I travel constantly, build out guides for every place I land, and share the fitness and lifestyle content that keeps me going between flights. This website is the home for all of it.
